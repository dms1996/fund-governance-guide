# Fund Setup and Onboarding Checklist

**Capital Management Co. -- Fund Governance Simulation**

This checklist governs the end-to-end process for onboarding a new sub-fund or standalone fund under the umbrella structure. Each task must be signed off by the responsible party before the next phase gate is opened.

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch

### 1.1 Regulatory Approval
- [ ] Determine fund domicile (Ireland / Luxembourg) and applicable regulatory framework (UCITS / AIFMD)
- [ ] Prepare and submit Central Bank of Ireland (CBI) or CSSF application package
- [ ] Obtain regulatory pre-clearance or no-objection letter
- [ ] File prospectus / supplement with the regulator for review
- [ ] Confirm KIID / KID (PRIIPs) requirements and prepare draft documents
- [ ] Register with relevant tax authorities (Irish Revenue, Luxembourg ACD)

### 1.2 Prospectus and Legal Documentation
- [ ] Draft fund supplement / prospectus (legal counsel)
- [ ] Define investment objective, policy, and restrictions
- [ ] Specify share classes (Institutional, Retail, Accumulating, Distributing)
- [ ] Define fee structure (management fee, performance fee, TER cap)
- [ ] Draft subscription and redemption forms
- [ ] Finalise risk disclosures and conflicts of interest section
- [ ] Legal review and sign-off by fund counsel

### 1.3 Board and Governance Approval
- [ ] Present fund proposal to the Board of Directors
- [ ] Board resolution approving the new fund launch
- [ ] Appoint designated person(s) responsible for the fund (ManCo)
- [ ] Confirm compliance officer sign-off on investment restrictions
- [ ] Confirm MLRO sign-off on AML/KYC onboarding procedures
- [ ] Record board minutes and file with company secretary

---

## Phase 2: Setup

### 2.1 Service Provider Agreements
- [ ] Execute or amend administration agreement (Administrator)
- [ ] Execute or amend depositary / custodian agreement
- [ ] Execute or amend investment management agreement (IMA)
- [ ] Confirm audit engagement letter with auditor
- [ ] Appoint transfer agent (if separate from administrator)
- [ ] Appoint distributor(s) and execute distribution agreements
- [ ] Confirm insurance / indemnity cover is extended to the new fund

### 2.2 System Configuration
- [ ] Set up fund on administrator's accounting platform (e.g., InvestOne, Fund Accounting System, HiPortfolio)
- [ ] Configure NAV calculation parameters (valuation points, pricing sources, rounding rules)
- [ ] Set up share class structures with correct ISIN codes
- [ ] Configure fee accrual schedules and crystallisation logic
- [ ] Set up bank accounts (subscription, redemption, expense, income)
- [ ] Register ISINs with Euroclear / Clearstream as applicable
- [ ] Configure Bloomberg / Reuters fund identifiers

### 2.3 Operational Readiness
- [ ] Complete AML/KYC due diligence on seed investor(s)
- [ ] Confirm dealing calendar and cut-off times
- [ ] Set up automated data feeds (pricing, FX rates, corporate actions)
- [ ] Test NAV calculation with sample portfolio (dry run)
- [ ] Validate reconciliation procedures between custodian and administrator
- [ ] Confirm disaster recovery and business continuity procedures cover the new fund

---

## Phase 3: Go-Live

### 3.1 NAV Calculation and First Dealing Day
- [ ] Process seed investment and confirm receipt of subscription monies
- [ ] Calculate first official NAV and verify against independent check
- [ ] Confirm NAV dissemination to data vendors (Bloomberg, Refinitiv, Morningstar)
- [ ] Process any Day 1 subscriptions and issue contract notes
- [ ] Confirm all regulatory filings are submitted (CBI Online Reporting, CSSF e-file)

### 3.2 Investor Onboarding
- [ ] Distribute finalised prospectus and KIID/KID to investors
- [ ] Complete AML/KYC for all initial subscribers
- [ ] Confirm investor classification (Professional, Retail, Eligible Counterparty)
- [ ] Issue investor welcome pack and portal credentials
- [ ] Confirm tax documentation (W-8BEN, self-certification) collected

### 3.3 Regulatory Filings
- [ ] File launch notification with CBI / CSSF
- [ ] Submit AIFMD Annex IV reporting template (if AIF)
- [ ] Register for CRS / FATCA reporting
- [ ] Notify relevant EU member states if passporting (UCITS Management Company Passport)
- [ ] Confirm LEI registration for the fund entity

---

## Phase 4: Post-Launch

### 4.1 Ongoing Monitoring (First 90 Days)
- [ ] Daily NAV review and break analysis for first 20 business days
- [ ] Weekly reconciliation sign-off (portfolio positions, cash, FX)
- [ ] Confirm expense accruals are in line with prospectus-disclosed fee caps
- [ ] Monitor investment restriction compliance daily (pre- and post-trade)
- [ ] Review swing pricing / anti-dilution levy triggers

### 4.2 First Board Report
- [ ] Prepare fund performance summary for first board meeting post-launch
- [ ] Report on AUM growth and investor concentration
- [ ] Present NAV break summary and resolution statistics
- [ ] Report on compliance monitoring exceptions (if any)
- [ ] Confirm all service provider SLAs are being met
- [ ] Submit post-launch lessons learned memo to the board

### 4.3 Ongoing Governance
- [ ] Schedule quarterly board reporting cycle
- [ ] Add fund to annual auditor planning schedule
- [ ] Include fund in ManCo risk management framework and RMP review
- [ ] Update fund catalogue on internal and client-facing systems
- [ ] Confirm ongoing regulatory reporting calendar is in place
